Thank you for playing Tankutz 2D. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the Game.

The Game stores gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

This information is stored solely to provide the Game's functionality and maintain your progress. This data remains on your device unless removed by uninstalling the Game or clearing the app's data.

The Game displays rewarded advertisements through the LevelPlay mediation platform.
Advertising partners may automatically collect certain information, including:

-Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)

-IP address

-Operating system information

-Language settings

-General location information derived from IP address

This information is used to provide, personalize (where permitted), measure, and improve advertising services.

*How We Use Information*

We use information to:

-Save and restore game progress

-Provide rewarded advertisements

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

You may be presented with a consent message that allows you to:

You may be able to change your advertising preferences at any time through available privacy options within the Game or through your device settings.

*Data Storage*

Game save data is stored locally on your Android device.
We do not operate servers that store your game progress, high scores, or in-game currency.

*Data Sharing*

We do not sell your personal information.
Information may be shared with advertising partners solely for the purposes of serving advertisements, measuring ad performance, preventing fraud, and complying with legal obligations.

*Children's Privacy*

The Game is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the Game. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
